# Design Guidelines

## The Logo

### Concept

**EDC** — three letters, bold and clean.  
**Cross** — hanging from the C like a pendant, a keychain, something carried daily.

The visual says: *This is something you keep with you. Every day.*

### Primary Logo

```
    E D C
      †
      
Every Day Cross
```

- **Type:** Bold sans-serif (Helvetica, Futura, Montserrat Bold)
- **Weight:** Uniform stroke, geometric, timeless
- **Cross:** Simple unadorned Latin cross, same stroke weight as letters
- **Connection:** Small loop/chain from bottom of C to cross top

### Versions

#### Horizontal (Primary)
EDC letters in a row, cross hanging from C, tagline underneath.

Use for: Headers, website banners, printed materials

#### Square (Social)
Stacked EDC with centered cross, no tagline.

Use for: Profile pictures, avatars, favicons

#### Icon Only
Just the cross pendant mark.

Use for: Watermarks, small icons, app icons

### Colors

| Use | Color | Hex |
|-----|-------|-----|
| Primary text | Charcoal | `#1a1a1a` |
| Background | White | `#ffffff` |
| Reverse text | White | `#ffffff` |
| Reverse background | Charcoal | `#1a1a1a` |
| Accent (optional) | Warm tan | `#c4a77d` |

Keep it simple. Black and white is your workhorse. Accent color for special moments only.

### Clear Space

Give the logo breathing room. No text or graphics within the height of the "E" on all sides.

### What Not to Do

❌ Don't stretch or distort the letters  
❌ Don't change the font to something decorative  
❌ Don't add shadows, gradients, or effects  
❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds without contrast  
❌ Don't separate the cross from the C
